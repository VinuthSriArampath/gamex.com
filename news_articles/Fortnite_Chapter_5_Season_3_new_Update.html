fortnite chapter 5 season 3 update news

Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3: Wrecked is here, turning the map into a post-apocalyptic wasteland, bringing new POIs, Nitro abilities, weapons, vehicle mods, and more. Here's everything you need to know about Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3.

Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3 - New POIs

  • Redline Rig
  • Nitrodome
  • Brutal Beachhead

New Weapons in Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3

There are new wasteland-themed weapons added to Chapter 5 Season 3: Wrecked, which include:

  • Nitro fists

    Nitro Fists have four charges that recharge once every eight seconds and allow you to mess your opponent's day up with a devastating 3x punch combo - think Doomfist from Overwatch. It also has an uppercut move and you can even dash through the air with it for extra movement. The 3x punch combo doesn't use any of the weapon's four charges, while the uppercut and aerial dash moves use one each. You can find Nitro Fists by defeating bosses or looting chests across the island. Its Mythical variant, Megalo Don's Nitro Fists, is obtained by defeating Megalo Don.

  • Boom Bolt

    The Boom Bolt is a crossbow that uses explosive bolts to deal damage. It fires fast, and is moddable at a Weapon Mod Bench. While you can hit any opponent with it, it's best used to immobilize vehicles, especially in areas like the Nitrodome. You can find Boom Bolts across the island, though you can only find its Mythical variant, Ringmaster's Boom Bolt, by defeating Ringmaster Scarr.

  • The Machinist's Combat Assault Rifle

    The Combat Assault Rifle returns to Fortnite, but in the form of a new Mythic that's dropped by The Machinist in Redline Rig. If you like a fast fire rate and a decent bullet spread, it's worth trying to take her down for.

New Vehicle - War Bus

The War Bus is a new vehicle in Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3. You can't just pick one up from anywhere on the island, though. There are only two to be found, both driven by patrollers you must find and take out before you can drive the War Bus.You'll find two Cannons atop the War Bus, perfect for taking out other on-road enemies. It also has an EMP Pulse-producing horn that temporarily disables vehicles in its path and damages nearby opponents' Shields.

Vehicle Mods in Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3

Vehicles play a big role in Chapter 5 Season 3, and there are several vehicle mods to help you survive for longer while on the road.

  • Cow Catcher - Gives the vehicle more armor and higher damage output.
  • Chonkers Off-Road Tires - Makes the vehicle easier to handle when off-road.
  • Machine Gun Turret - Adds a turret to the vehicle's roof that can be fired by another player in multiplayer matches.
  • Grenade Launcher Turret - Adds a grenade-launching turret to the vehicle's roof.
  • Spiked Bumper - Gives the vehicle a higher damage output.
  • Bulletproof Tires - Stops bullets from bursting the vehicle's tires.

    • You can also repair any damage to your vehicle using a Repair Box or Repair Torch, both found across the island.

New Healing Items in Chapter 5 Season 3

Two new healing items have arrived in Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 3: Nuka-Cola and Kind Cacti. Yes, Nuka-Cola, the famous Fallout beverage, which you'll find across the island in chests and boxes.Kind Cacti isn't a consumable item; rather, you must find a Slurp Cactus on the island and either pickaxe it or drive into it with a vehicle to release its healing properties.
